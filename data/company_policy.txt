Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid leave annually. Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 10 days. Sick leave does not require prior approval but must be reported within 24 hours.

Work From Home Policy:
Employees can work from home up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Full-time remote work is not permitted unless explicitly approved by HR.

Travel Policy:
All business travel must be approved in advance. Economy class should be booked for domestic travel. Reimbursement claims must be submitted within 7 days of travel completion.

Code of Conduct:
Employees are expected to maintain professionalism and respect in all workplace interactions. Harassment or discrimination will not be tolerated and may lead to disciplinary action.

Expense Reimbursement Policy:
Expenses must be work-related and supported by valid receipts. Reimbursement requests should be submitted within 10 days.

Data Security Policy:
Employees must not share confidential company data externally. All devices must be secured with passwords. Unauthorized access or data breaches must be reported immediately.
